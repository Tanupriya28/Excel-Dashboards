# Excel Dashboards

A collection of business-focused Excel dashboards built using Pivot Tables, calculated fields, and advanced data visualization techniques.  
These dashboards demonstrate strong skills in data analysis, KPI design, and executive-level reporting.

---

## Dashboards Included

### 1. E-commerce Sales Dashboard
**Objective:**  
Analyze overall e-commerce performance across sales, profit, discounts, customers, and product categories.

**Key Insights & Features:**
- KPI cards for Sales, Orders, Quantity, and Profit
- Profit Margin by Category (identifies loss-making categories)
- Category-wise Sales & Profit comparison
- Sales vs Discount impact on Quantity
- Monthly Sales & Profit trends
- Customer Demographics analysis
- Top 10 Customers by Sales

### 2. Swiggy Instamart Sales Dashboard
**Objective:**  
Provide a business intelligence view of grocery & instant-delivery operations focusing on outlet performance, item mix, and customer behavior.

**Key Insights & Features:**
- Total Sales, Average Sales, Average Rating, and Item Count KPIs
- Outlet Establishment trend over time
- Sales analysis by Outlet Size and Location Tier
- Item Type contribution to total sales
- Fat Content analysis (Low Fat vs Regular)
- Outlet Type comparison (Supermarkets vs Grocery Stores)
- Interactive slicers for dynamic filtering
